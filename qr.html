QR Menus for Tables

Unique QR per table → scan, order, and the POS gets the order instantly.

How it works

  1. Each table has a unique QR code with its table number.
  2. Guests scan → see menu on their phone → place order.
  3. Order appears on the bar/kitchen POS with the correct table.
  4. Staff confirms/serves. No waiting, faster turnover.

Why it’s great

  • Less walking for staff, more attention where it matters.
  • Guests order immediately — no waiting for a waiter.
  • Upsell with photos, combos, and specials.
  • Clean itemized bill by table for easy checkout.
